      About Gridiron Give

      Gridiron Give connects donors with athletes who are raising funds for sport-specific equipment. Coaches manage rosters, players set up goal items, and donors can support exact needs in one place.

      Terms of Service

      By using Gridiron Give, users agree to provide accurate information, respect platform rules, and use funds for intended equipment purposes. Donations are voluntary, and account misuse may result in access removal.

      Privacy Policy

      Gridiron Give stores account and donation information to operate the platform. Contact and profile data are used for core functionality such as account setup, notifications, and donation records.
